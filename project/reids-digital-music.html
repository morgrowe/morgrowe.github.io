Reid's Digital Music

May 2013 | Website design

Reid's Digital Music provides computer music tuition for people of all ages and abilites. The business required a new website that was able to accept online payments and allow the business owner to update it easily.

The website's custom theme was implimented flawlessly with WordPress. This allowed the company to have a unique looking website with all the powerful features that WordPress provides. Some of the features include a configurable image slider, a blogging engine, custom pages and various custom modules, which allow automatic information retrieval from all over the website. No coding knowledge is needed to maintain this website.

Posters, fliers and banners were designed and printed, allowing the business to advertise itself during presentations and events.

Feel free to visit Reid's Digital Music's live website.

Technologies Used

  • HTML 5
  • CSS 3
  • JavaScript (jQuery)
  • WordPress (PHP, MySQL)
    • Custom Post Types
    • Advanced Custom Fields
    • Custom WPLoops
  • PayPal integration
